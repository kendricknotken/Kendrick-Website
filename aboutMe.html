Level Designer | Game Designer

A selfie I took of my Grandpa and I celebrating his 60th wedding aniversary :)

I'm Kendrick Tran, and I am a game designer who specializes in level design. I use pen, pencil, 2D, and 3D modeling tools to prototype and demonstrate my ideas across multiple iterations. I started designing games when I swapped to the Game Design major my second semester of college and started learning my first language (C++) my junior year of college

I have always had a passion for games and have been playing for as long as I remember. The earliest systems I remember using are the Nintendo DS, Playstation 2, and Wii. I love designing games, and it is my goal is to create an experience that engulfs the player into a world that makes them curious and excited to explore.

My favorite games are Dying Light 2, League of Legends, Detroit: Become Human, Minecraft, Valheim, STAR WARS Jedi: Fallen Order, NEW WORLD BETA, and Core Keeper

I'm Grandmaster in League of Legends and Diamond in Valorant

I was born in Nashville, TN and raised just half an hour south in the town of Nolensville. I've played a variety of sports, but the one that always stuck with me was soccer which i played for 13 years. The people I love the most are my Mom, Dad, and brother who have supported me my whole life. I would not be where I am today without them.

My family and I also have three cats and the one I look after is named Blackie, who I got back in 2009 when I was 8 years old and he was only a couple weeks old.

He enjoys sleeping and meowing
