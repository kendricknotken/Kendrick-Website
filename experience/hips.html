Overview

Duration: Jaunary 2023 - Ongoing
Developed by: North Carolina Department of Public Safety
Tools: UE5, Github, Trello
Roles: Level Designer, Game Designer

Description: The goal of the game is to teach player's about the signs
early drug use. Player's are placed in a what seems like a regular teenager's
bedroom, but around them are hidden compartments and items. It is up to
the player to find these secrets that are hidden in plain sight.

Responsibilities:

  • Central Vision Holder and decision maker for levels
  • Responsible for documenting research, planning, and design processes
  • Understood project requirements and individual responsibilities through an agile lens
  • Engaged in productive conversation/problem solving during team scrum activities
  • Responsible for grey boxing, prototyping, and mapping levels
  • Collaborated closely with programmers and artists
  • Created base 3D assets for design consistency
